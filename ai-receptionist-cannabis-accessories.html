Can it verify age or sell age-restricted products?

No. It answers specs, materials, compatibility, and stock for your retail and online catalog, and checks order, shipping, and return status. It never verifies age and never completes an age-restricted sale: that stays at your counter and your checkout. The agent identifies itself on every call and routes anything outside its rails to your staff.

Does it capture wholesale and B2B leads?

Yes. It recognizes a bulk or wholesale inquiry, captures quantities, business details, and timeline, and routes it to your rep to close, so high-value B2B calls don't die in a voicemail box. The agent doesn't negotiate terms or quote wholesale pricing: it takes the full intake and hands your rep a lead ready to call back.

Does it integrate with Shopify or Square?

We wire the managed-agent rails into the store platform you already run, including Shopify, Square, WooCommerce, Lightspeed, and Clover. The agent reads your live catalog and order data, not a separate copy.
